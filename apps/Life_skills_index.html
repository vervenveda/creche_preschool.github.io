Step 1 · Clean hands
First, we wash our hands.
Today I get to help in the kitchen! Before I touch any food, I roll up my sleeves and wash my hands with warm water and soap. I scrub, scrub, scrub, then rinse and dry. Now my hands are clean and ready to help.
Grown-up note: Let your child practice turning on water, using soap, and drying hands with a small towel they can reach.
