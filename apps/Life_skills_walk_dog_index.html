Walking With a Dog Safely

A gentle story about preparation, kindness, body language, traffic awareness, and caring for a dog with a trusted grown-up.

Important: A young child should never walk a dog alone or remain responsible for controlling the leash. A trusted adult handles the leash, traffic, route, weather, greetings, and emergencies. Children help by observing, listening, and practicing kindness.
