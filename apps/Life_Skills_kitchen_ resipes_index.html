Little Kitchen Recipes

Simple food activities with clear adult-help labels, allergen reminders, and age-appropriate texture and choking notes.

Important: “Kid-safe” never means unsupervised. A trusted adult must check allergies, food temperature, freshness, choking risks, and age-appropriate preparation. Whole grapes, hard raw produce, popcorn, nuts, skewers, toothpicks, chewy bagels, and frozen-hard foods can be dangerous for young children. Honey is not for children under 12 months.
No-Cook
🥣
Wash hands first. Ask before tasting. Stop and tell a grown-up if food smells unusual, feels too hot, or causes itching, swelling, coughing, or trouble breathing.
